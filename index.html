About

I make custom epoxy tables that combine woodworking skills with unique resin designs. Each table is one of a kind, created for people who want something custom and high quality. I started building epoxy tables in my garage as a hobby and have continued ever since, experimenting with different designs, learning new techniques, and improving my skills with every piece I make. Every table I build reflects the time and effort I put into it, and I take pride in creating something that is both functional and visually striking.
