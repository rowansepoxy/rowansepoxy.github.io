About

RJ Epoxy Tables was founded by me and my friend John with a shared passion for woodworking and resin design. We specialize in creating custom epoxy tables that are both functional and visually striking, combining craftsmanship with unique, one-of-a-kind designs. Each piece is carefully constructed with attention to detail and quality, reflecting our commitment to excellence and innovation. From our first pieces built as a hobby to our current projects, we continue to explore new techniques and push the boundaries of what is possible with epoxy and wood.
